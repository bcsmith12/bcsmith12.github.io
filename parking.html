Parking

Where to Park

Option 1: Behind building at the corner of Main and Midland/E. Vine (formerly People’s Exchange Bank)

Option 2: Lot to the left of Awesome Inc building

Option 3: Metered spots behind the building (on Water St.) or the metered spots in front of the building (on Main St.)

Option 4:Outside the retail plaza (in front of garage)

Do Not Park:

Parking Garage - across the street, under “Main and Vine” apartment/condo complex.

Spaces marked for Wells Fargo, Commerce Lexington, Porter Paints or VisitLex
